About

Engineering reliability from the inside out.

A highly accomplished Leadership‑stage automotive professional with over 18 years of comprehensive experience spanning vehicle validation, electrical and mechanical design, and high‑voltage system testing for commercial vehicles. Currently thriving as Program Lead for Electrical Validation and Testing of Class 8 Commercial Trucks in a dynamic product development environment. Expertise includes developing and executing rigorous test plans covering signal integrity, power delivery analysis, and PCB validation — consistently driving design compliance and accelerated issue resolution. Proficient in CAN Network Diagnostics (SAE J1939) using Vector CANalyzer for prototype debugging, diagnostic troubleshooting, and vehicle shakedown tests. Proven track record in Cross‑Functional Team Leadership (managing 20 Mechanical Design Engineers) and applying structured quality methodologies including SAFe Scrum Master and DFSS Green Belt. Dedicated to delivering ATS‑optimized test solutions and ensuring functional safety compliance across vehicle systems architecture.
