02 — Origin

Twenty years, three industries, one thread: making complex systems legible.

The path started in Bangalore, in telecom infrastructure at Infosys — DNS, Kerberos, RADIUS, and mail systems for a large-scale telecommunications environment, and signaling systems where a bug meant a dropped call, not a dashboard alert. From there, batch automation for a healthcare clinical infrastructure project at Cigna. Then FIS: six years of Level 2 production support for consumer online banking used by hundreds of financial institutions, before moving onto the team behind Infinity IDP — the identity platform quietly verifying who's who for banks, payment processors, and capital markets clients around the world.

B.E., Computer Science — Visvesvaraya Technological University, 2002–2006
