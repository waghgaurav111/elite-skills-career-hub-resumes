About

The translator between business intent and technical delivery.

Mausami Wagh is a Technical Product Owner and Senior Business/Data Analyst with 10+ years of experience across banking, payments, insurance, healthcare, publishing and construction technology. She turns complex stakeholder needs into prioritized epics, user stories, acceptance criteria, process models and technical specifications. Her work spans roadmap execution, PI planning, API integration, SQL analysis, data migration, UAT and post-production verification. Across product, engineering, QA, data, compliance, vendor and leadership groups, she brings disciplined risk management, dependency resolution, release readiness and data-informed decision-making to each delivery cycle.
